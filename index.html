About us

Our company was born far, far away in Siberia, Russia. Despite the fact that our team is still small, we have achieved impressive results. Since 2018, our apps have been downloaded over 100,000,000 times. The daily audience is more than 1,000,000 users. We manage 10 apps and are constantly working on new ones. We strive to make each new project better and more interesting than the previous one. All members of the sect work remotely and are located in different locations.

Our core values: freedom, development, involvement, love for what we do. But we’re still at the beginning of our journey.

We want not just to make the world a better place. We want to change it completely, and we know how to do it.
